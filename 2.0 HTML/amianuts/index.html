Amia Nuts

The company

Amia nuts are native to the Amazon rainforest and are a good source of protein, healthy fats, and minerals such as magnesium, phosphorus, and zinc. Amia nuts are often used as a snack or added to dishes such as salads, trail mixes, and desserts. However, it's worth noting that some people may have allergies to amia nuts, so it's important to be aware of this if you or anyone you know is planning to consume them.
